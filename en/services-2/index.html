Front desk & Check in

Hotel Belley does not have an on-site reception. Our team is located in our neighboring hotel!

We will be waiting for you at Hôtel Le Saint-Paul for your check-in and check-out.

Parking lot

We do not offer valet service. However, we work with nearby parking offering you unlimited entries and exits. Additional charge added to your invoice.

Front desk closed at night / emergencies

Our front desk is open from 7 a.m. to 11 p.m.

During closing hours, we have our Hotel Le Priori team available to answer your questions.

Breakfast

Unfortunately, we do not offer breakfast. Do not hesitate to ask our front desk team, they will be delighted to suggest you places around the hotel.

Taverne Belley

Lively atmosphere in the evenings! The sounds of the tavern could echo into the night.

Accessibility

The Hôtel Belley does not have an elevator. Our rooms are located on the 1st and 2nd floor, only accessible by staircase.
